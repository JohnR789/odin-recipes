Steak Marsala

Description

Steak Marsala is a savory Italian dish featuring beef cooked in a rich Marsala wine sauce.

Originating from Sicily, Marsala wine is used in this classic Italian recipe.

Ingredients

  • 4 boneless beef steaks (such as sirloin or ribeye)
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon unsalted butter
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 8 ounces cremini or button mushrooms, sliced
  • 1 cup Marsala wine
  • 1 cup beef broth
  • 1/4 cup heavy cream (optional for a richer sauce)
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley, chopped (for garnish)

Steps

  1. Prepare the Steaks:
    • Season the steaks on both sides with salt and pepper.
  2. Cook the Steaks:
    • In a large skillet, heat the olive oil over medium-high heat.
    • Add the steaks and cook for about 3-4 minutes per side for medium-rare, or until they reach your desired doneness.
    • Remove the steaks from the skillet and set them aside on a plate. Cover them loosely with foil to keep warm.
  3. Saut√© the Vegetables:
    • In the same skillet, add the butter.
    • Once the butter is melted, add the chopped onion and cook until softened, about 3 minutes.
    • Add the minced garlic and sliced mushrooms. Cook until the mushrooms are browned and tender, about 5 minutes.
  4. Make the Marsala Sauce:
    • Pour in the Marsala wine, scraping up any browned bits from the bottom of the skillet.
    • Let the wine simmer for about 2-3 minutes to reduce slightly.
    • Add the beef broth and bring the mixture to a simmer. Allow it to cook for another 5 minutes, reducing the sauce slightly.
    • If using, stir in the heavy cream for a richer sauce and simmer for an additional 2 minutes.
  5. Combine and Serve:
    • Return the steaks to the skillet, spooning some of the sauce over them.
    • Cook for another 1-2 minutes, just to heat the steaks through and meld the flavors.
    • Garnish with chopped fresh parsley.
  6. Serve:
    • Serve the Steak Marsala with your favorite sides, such as mashed potatoes, roasted vegetables, or pasta.

Enjoy your flavorful and elegant Steak Marsala!
